Seminar 3

Britain from the Late Middle Ages to Present

  1. Read the material from the textbooks indicated below and be ready to give brief talks on the following issues.
    1. The Tudor age:
      1. 1.1. The reign of Henry VII.
      2. 1.2. The English Reformation.
      3. 1.3. Queen Elizabeth. The period of Renaissance.
    2. Britain in the 17th century:
      1. 2.1. The Civil War.
      2. 2.2. The Commonwealth.
      3. 2.3. The restoration of monarchy.
      4. 2.4. The Glorious Revolution and the Bill of Rights.
    3. Britain in the 18th–19th centuries:
      1. 3.1. The Industrial Revolution and the rise of the middle class.
      2. 3.2. The Victorian era, social and economic improvements.
      3. 3.3. The growth of the British Empire (colonial expansion).
    4. Britain in the 20th century:
      1. 4.1. The end of the Empire.
      2. 4.2. The First World War.
      3. 4.3. The Second World War.
      4. 4.4. The ‘welfare state’.
      5. 4.5. Thatcherism.
    5. The United Kingdom today.
  2. Choose a topic for your project work and prepare a presentation. Think over the questions for discussion at class.
    1. Henry VIII as a personality and a monarch.
    2. The English Renaissance as the period of great personalities.
    3. Victorian technology, inventions and innovation.
    4. Sir Winston Churchill.
